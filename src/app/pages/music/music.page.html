Music

Before I learned how to code, my main creative outlet was music.

I learned to play guitar when I was a teenager. While I was in high school, I started a rock band with a few of my friends. I developed a preference for the bass guitar, and therefore took on the role of the bassist in the group.

We found it difficult to find a lead singer, so I decided to assume that role as well. It was mostly because I had a strong desire to write and perform my own songs. My band carried on into my college years under a new name, but with mostly the same lineup.
